In a prescient future driven by genetic engineering,

the course of a human life is pre-determined at birth

by DNA. In Gattaca, Vincent, a product of natural

reproduction attempts to subvert his genetic destiny

and realize his ambitions of being a Saturn-bound

astronaut through biological masquerades.
